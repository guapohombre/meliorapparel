Founder

Laura Tempesta

Laura is the only person in North America with a master's degree in lingerie design. She is the founder of Bravolution®, a consultancy dedicated to enhancing bra design for major brands while educating consumers.

While working in the apparel industry, Laura fit bras more than 1,500 times and learned about the pain, discomfort, and shame women feel when wearing bras. She shared their frustration.

In 2019, Laura shared her bra knowledge in a TEDx Talk that has since been viewed by over 3 million people.

Laura found that folks in the apparel industry weren't willing to take risks and embrace her ideas to replace the bra - or at least make the bra radically better. This motivated her to start her own apparel company focused and comfort and beauty.
